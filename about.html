About PDF Evaluator

Welcome to PDF Evaluator, an application designed to analyze PDF documents and extract various information from them.

Our tool allows you to upload a PDF file and retrieve data such as word count, information about images, tables, fonts, font sizes, and a summary of the document.

PDF Evaluator is equipped with advanced algorithms to parse PDFs efficiently, enabling users to gain insights quickly and easily.

Whether you're a student, researcher, professional, or simply curious about the contents of a PDF document, our application aims to provide you with valuable information and analysis.

Features:

  • Word count analysis
  • Identification of images and tables
  • Font and font size recognition
  • Language detection
  • Summary generation
